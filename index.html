🗼 Paris 1889

Location: Paris, France

Era: 1889 - The Belle Époque

Experience one of history's most transformative moments. Watch the Eiffel Tower rise from the ground during the 1889 World's Fair. Stroll through the cobblestone streets, enjoy impressionist art at Montmartre, and witness the birth of modern urban design.

Highlights:

  • Eiffel Tower construction
  • World's Fair (Exposition Universelle)
  • Montmartre artist community
  • Belle Époque architecture
